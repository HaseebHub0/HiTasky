## Full description  (max 4000)
```
HiTasky is a quiet, beautiful to-do app for people who want to get things done
without the noise. No account. No cloud. No ads. Everything lives on your phone —
and nowhere else.

Most task apps shout at you. HiTasky doesn't. It's built around a single calm
question: what do I do now? Your most important task sits front and centre, and
finishing it feels good — a soft haptic tick and an ink-strike line, like closing
a fountain pen.

WHY YOU'LL LIKE IT

• Truly private — your tasks, lists and notes never leave your device. There's no
  sign-up and no server holding your data.
• Works fully offline — open it on a plane, in a tunnel, anywhere.
• No ads, no tracking, no subscription.
• A calm, editorial design with a warm dark theme and a clean paper-light theme.

WHAT'S INSIDE

• Today view — your focus for the day, with your "Now" task highlighted.
• Lists — group tasks by project (Work, Home, Reading… your call), each with its
  own colour and icon.
• Reminders — gentle, on-device notifications. Pick a separate reminder time so
  you're nudged exactly when it matters.
• Repeats — daily, weekdays, or weekly tasks that come back on their own.
• Search — find any task across every list in a tap.
• Journal — a quiet history of what you've finished, with streaks and a weekly
  activity chart.
• Home-screen widget — today's tasks, right on your launcher.
• Local backup & restore — export a file whenever you like; you decide where it goes.
• Haptics & a satisfying completion animation that make finishing tasks feel real.

HiTasky is free. No account, no catch — just a calmer way to keep your days.

Your data stays yours.
```
