# Privacy Policy — HiTasky

**Effective date:** 2 June 2026
**App:** HiTasky (Android)
**Developer contact:** muhammadhaseeb9323@gmail.com

HiTasky is built to be private by default. This policy explains, in plain words,
what happens to your information.

## The short version

- Your **tasks, lists, and settings stay on your phone**. We don't have an account
  system, a server for your data, or any cloud sync.
- We **don't show ads**, and we **don't use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs**.
- The **only** time anything leaves your device is when **you choose to send feedback**
  from Settings — and even then we don't ask for your name or email.

## 1. Data stored on your device

Everything you create in HiTasky — tasks, lists, due dates, reminders, completed
history, and your preferences (theme, accent, haptics) — is saved **locally on your
device** using the app's on-device storage. It is not transmitted to us or anyone else.

If you uninstall the app, this data is removed with it.

## 2. Feedback you choose to send

HiTasky has an optional "Share your feedback" feature (and an occasional in-app prompt).
If — and only if — you choose to submit feedback, the following is sent to the developer:

- the **mood** you selected,
- the **comment text** you wrote,
- the **date/time**, the **app version**, your **platform** (Android), and your **theme**.

We do **not** collect your name, email, device ID, contacts, location, or the contents of
your tasks. This feedback is stored in a private Google Sheet (via Google Apps Script),
accessible only to the developer, and is used solely to improve the app. It is not sold or
shared with advertisers.

A copy of your submitted feedback is also kept locally on your device.

## 3. Notifications

If you enable reminders, notifications are scheduled and shown **locally on your device**.
No reminder content is sent to any server.

## 4. Backups

The backup/restore feature is fully manual. When you export a backup, a file is created
and you decide where it goes via your device's share sheet. Restoring reads a file you
pick. These files are never uploaded to us automatically.

## 5. What we do NOT do

- We do **not** sell or rent your data.
- We do **not** run ads or ad networks.
- We do **not** embed analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs in the app.
- We do **not** require an account or log-in.
- We do **not** track you across apps or websites.

## 6. Third-party services

- **Google Play** delivers and updates the app and may collect limited information
  (such as aggregate install and crash statistics) under
  [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). This is standard for all
  Android apps and is not something HiTasky adds.
- **Google Sheets / Google Apps Script** is used only to receive feedback you choose to
  send (see Section 2).

## 7. Children's privacy

HiTasky is a general-purpose productivity app and is not directed at children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 8. Data retention and deletion

- **On-device data:** removed when you uninstall the app, or via Settings → "Reset all data".
- **Feedback:** to have a feedback entry you sent removed from our records, email
  **muhammadhaseeb9323@gmail.com** and we will delete it.

## 9. Security

Because your data lives on your own device, its security is tied to your device's security
(screen lock, encryption, etc.). Feedback is transmitted over HTTPS.

## 10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the "Effective date" above and post the new version at
the same URL.

## 11. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email **muhammadhaseeb9323@gmail.com**.
