HiTasky — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2 June 2026 · Android · Contact: muhammadhaseeb9323@gmail.com

HiTasky is built to be private by default. This policy explains, in plain words, what happens to your information.

The short version

Your tasks, lists, and settings stay on your phone. There is no account and no cloud sync. We don't show ads and we don't use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. The only time anything leaves your device is when you choose to send feedback — and even then we don't ask for your name or email.

1. Data stored on your device

Everything you create — tasks, lists, due dates, reminders, completed history, and your preferences (theme, accent, haptics) — is saved locally on your device. It is not transmitted to us or anyone else. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

2. Feedback you choose to send

HiTasky has an optional "Share your feedback" feature (and an occasional in-app prompt). If — and only if — you choose to submit feedback, the following is sent to the developer: the mood you selected, the comment text you wrote, the date/time, app version, platform, and theme.

We do not collect your name, email, device ID, contacts, location, or the contents of your tasks. Feedback is stored in a private Google Sheet (via Google Apps Script), accessible only to the developer, used solely to improve the app, and never sold or shared with advertisers. A copy is also kept locally on your device.

3. Notifications

If you enable reminders, notifications are scheduled and shown locally on your device. No reminder content is sent to any server.

4. Backups

Backup/restore is fully manual. Exporting creates a file you place yourself via your device's share sheet; restoring reads a file you pick. These files are never uploaded to us automatically.

5. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or rent your data.
  • We do not run ads or ad networks.
  • We do not embed analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not require an account or log-in.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.

6. Third-party services

Google Play delivers and updates the app and may collect limited information (such as aggregate install and crash statistics) under Google's Privacy Policy — standard for all Android apps. Google Sheets / Apps Script is used only to receive feedback you choose to send (Section 2).

7. Children's privacy

HiTasky is a general-purpose productivity app, not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Data retention and deletion

On-device data is removed when you uninstall the app or use Settings → "Reset all data". To have feedback you sent removed from our records, email muhammadhaseeb9323@gmail.com.

9. Security

Because your data lives on your own device, its security is tied to your device's security. Feedback is transmitted over HTTPS.

10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the effective date above and post the new version at the same URL.

11. Contact

Questions? Email muhammadhaseeb9323@gmail.com.
